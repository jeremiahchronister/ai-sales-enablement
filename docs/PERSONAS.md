# User Personas: AI Sales Enablement Platform

## Overview

This document defines the **4 primary user personas** for the AI Sales Enablement Platform. Understanding these personas drives product prioritization, UX design, and go-to-market strategy.

**Persona Development Method:**
- 25+ customer interviews (VP Sales, sales reps, RevOps leaders)
- Analysis of 50+ sales enablement platforms' user reviews
- Data from pilot customers (10 companies, 750 sales reps)

## Persona 1: Sarah Chen - The Quota-Driven Sales Rep

### Profile
- **Role:** Account Executive (AE) at SaaS company
- **Age:** 28
- **Experience:** 3 years in sales
- **Team Size:** 15 reps reporting to 1 manager
- **Quota:** $1.2M ARR annually
- **Typical Day:** 50+ emails, 10+ calls, 2-3 demos, 1-2 proposals

### Goals
1. **Hit quota every quarter** (primary motivation)
2. Spend more time selling, less time on admin work
3. Close deals faster (shorter sales cycles)
4. Increase win rate from 18% to 25%
5. Get promoted to Senior AE

### Pain Points
- **Wastes 40% of time on unqualified leads** - "I spend hours on leads that never close"
- **Proposal creation takes 4-8 hours** - "I copy-paste from old proposals and still miss key points"
- **Loses competitive deals** - "I didn't know competitor X had a new feature until it was too late"
- **Manager asks for call summaries** - "I have to manually type notes after every call"
- **CRM data entry is tedious** - "I spend 1 hour/day updating Salesforce"

### Use Cases

**1. Lead Qualification (Daily)**
- **Before:** Manually researches company on LinkedIn, checks industry fit, guesses budget
- **With Platform:** Uploads lead ‚Üí AI scores in 30 seconds ‚Üí Focuses on high-score leads only
- **Impact:** Saves 2 hours/day, increases qualified lead focus by 3x

**2. Proposal Generation (Weekly)**
- **Before:** Opens old proposal ‚Üí Find/replace company names ‚Üí 4 hours of editing
- **With Platform:** Enters lead info + deal size ‚Üí AI generates custom proposal in 3 minutes
- **Impact:** Saves 3.5 hours per proposal, 14 hours/month

**3. Competitive Objection Handling (Every Deal)**
- **Before:** Googles competitor during call ‚Üí Fumbles response ‚Üí Loses credibility
- **With Platform:** Opens battle card mid-call ‚Üí Reads talking points ‚Üí Confident response
- **Impact:** 20% higher win rate on competitive deals

**4. Call Analysis (After Every Meeting)**
- **Before:** Types scattered notes ‚Üí Forgets to log objections ‚Üí Manager can't coach
- **With Platform:** Uploads call recording ‚Üí AI extracts action items, objections, sentiment
- **Impact:** Better follow-up emails, manager coaching improves

### Success Metrics
- **Time to qualify lead:** 10 min ‚Üí 2 min (80% reduction)
- **Proposals per week:** 2 ‚Üí 5 (150% increase)
- **Win rate:** 18% ‚Üí 22% (22% improvement)
- **Quota attainment:** 85% ‚Üí 105% (becomes top performer)

### Preferred Features
1. ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **AI Lead Scoring** (saves most time)
2. ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Proposal Generation** (biggest win rate impact)

4. ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Conversation Intelligence** (manager likes it, but Sarah finds it less urgent)

## Anti-Personas (Not Our Target)

### ‚ùå Enterprise Sales Teams (1,000+ Reps)
**Why Not:**
- Need enterprise-grade security (SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, etc.) - we're not ready yet
- Complex procurement (6-12 month sales cycles) - we can't afford long sales cycles
- Custom SLAs and on-prem deployments - our cloud-only model doesn't fit

**Maybe in Year 3:** After we achieve SOC 2 compliance and have enterprise customers as references

### ‚ùå Solo Sales Consultants (1-5 Person Teams)
**Why Not:**
- Too price-sensitive ($150/user/month is expensive for solo consultant)
- Don't need team collaboration features
- Prefer simpler, cheaper tools (e.g., $20/month point solutions)

**Alternative Offer:** Freemium tier (5 users free, limited features)

### ‚ùå Non-Sales Teams (Customer Success, Marketing)
**Why Not:**
- Product is sales-specific (lead scoring, proposals, battle cards)
- Different workflows (CSMs don't do cold calls)
- Would require significant product changes

**Maybe in Year 2:** Expand to customer success (renewal proposals, upsell scoring)
